Welcome to CooKING, before we start we would like you to ask you for some information.

Cooking includes a voice control, to see the available commands, see the list below:

Available commands

  • Go to ... - say "go to my shopping list" to go to your shopping list, this also works for the other menus!
  • Search ... - say "search pancakes" to search for pancakes! (or say anything else you are searching for!
  • Select ... - say "select garlic" to indicate that you have garlic, this also works for any other ingredient!
  • Search by ingredients - say "search by ingredients" to search by the ingredients you have available
  • Add to shopping list - say this to add the ingredients of the current recipe to your shopping list! (You can also say "add from last recipe")
  • Clear ingredients - say this to clear your list of available ingredients
  • Clear shopping list - say this to clear your shopping list
  • Next step - say this to go to the next step in the current recipe
  • Previous step - say this to go to the previous step in the current recipe
  • Next recipe - say this to go to the next recipe in the search results
  • Previous recipe - say this to go to the previous recipe in the search results
